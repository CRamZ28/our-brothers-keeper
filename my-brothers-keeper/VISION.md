# Vision

> *"Grief lasts longer than meals. Grief lasts longer than flowers. Grief lasts longer than attention spans. Support should too."*

---

## Where We're Going

Our Brother's Keeper exists to build digital infrastructure that helps communities sustain love over time.

Not productivity tools. Not another notification to dismiss. Not a platform optimized for engagement.

Infrastructure. The kind that makes it easier for a neighbor to show up six months after the funeral than it was in the first week. The kind that makes a widow feel remembered on a Tuesday afternoon in November, two years later. The kind that holds space for grief without rushing it toward closure.

The goal is not efficiency. The goal is endurance.

---

## What We Want to Prevent

**No widow abandoned after month three.**

The first wave of support is almost always good. People come. They cook. They call. But support without structure fades. Not because people are heartless — because they're human, and humans forget, and life doesn't pause.

OBK is built to solve the forgetting problem. Not by guilt. Not by gamification. But by giving the support community a place to stay organized and a reason to return.

**No child growing up unremembered by community.**

One of the quiet tragedies of loss is the way it ripples forward. A father dies, and his children's birthdays go unremarked by the community that came to the funeral. The baseball games go unwatched. The milestones go uncelebrated by anyone outside immediate family.

Those children deserve to grow up knowing they were not forgotten.

**No family navigating long-term grief alone.**

The immediate aftermath of loss often brings more help than a family knows what to do with. Month seven is a different story. Year two is a different story. OBK exists to bridge that gap — to make it easy for a community to stay present when presence matters most and is hardest to sustain.

---

## What This Is Not

OBK is not:

- A social media platform
- A fundraising tool
- A grief counseling service
- A product built to monetize loss
- A system optimized for growth or engagement metrics

OBK is a tool. A private, intentional tool that reduces friction between people who want to help and a family that needs it. Technology in service of human presence — not a substitute for it.

---

## The Longer View

Right now, OBK is a web application built by one non-developer with conviction, AI tools, and a personal reason that made it impossible to stop.

The longer view is a platform that:

- Runs reliably for families across faith backgrounds, demographics, and life circumstances
- Expands to mobile without sacrificing privacy or simplicity
- Has community-driven feature development guided by the principles in [PHILOSOPHY.md](PHILOSOPHY.md)
- Is maintained by people who understand that this project exists to serve people in the worst moments of their lives

The goal is not to build a company. The goal is to build something that outlasts any one person's involvement — a tool so grounded in its purpose that it couldn't drift from it without the community noticing.

---

## The Standard

Every major decision about this project — every feature, every architectural choice, every partnership — should be held against a single question:

**Does this help a grieving family feel less alone, longer?**

If yes, it belongs. If not, it doesn't.
