Rendering/legibility tweaks

Ensure the sidebar text is readable over the gradient:

Nav item labels: text-white/90 (active: text-white)

Active pill: bg-[#B08CA7]/40 backdrop-blur-md border border-white/25

Add high-dpi crispness:

For PNG, export at 2× or 3× and size down with CSS.

Prefer SVG to avoid blur on scaling.

Accessibility

alt="Our Brother’s Keeper" on the logo image.

If the wordmark is purely decorative (the app name also appears as text elsewhere), add aria-hidden="true" on the wordmark image.

Acceptance Criteria

Logo appears centered at the top of the sidebar with a soft teal halo/glow.

Wordmark sits directly beneath (small-caps style with slightly larger first letters).

No visible background box around the images (true transparency).

Crisp at all sidebar widths; no fuzziness on HiDPI screens.
